1. WELCOME & COMPANY OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Welcome to ACME Corporation. This handbook provides guidance on your rights,
responsibilities, and the policies that govern employment at ACME.

ACME Corporation is a global technology and business services company with
operations in 42 countries, employing over 28,000 professionals worldwide.
Our headquarters are in San Francisco, California, with regional centres in
London, Singapore, Dubai, and Bengaluru.

Mission: To empower organisations through intelligent technology solutions.
Vision: To be the most trusted enterprise AI partner by 2027.

2. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 Employment At-Will
Employment at ACME is at-will for US-based employees, meaning either
the employee or the company may terminate employment at any time.

2.2 Equal Opportunity Employment
ACME is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on
race, colour, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability,
sexual orientation, or veteran status.

2.3 Probationary Period
New employees serve a 90-day probationary period. During this time,
performance is closely monitored. Extensions to the probationary period
may be granted at the discretion of the hiring manager.

3. WORKING HOURS & ATTENDANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Standard Hours
Standard working hours are 09:00–17:30 Monday to Friday (37.5 hours/week).
Flexible working arrangements may be agreed with your line manager.

3.2 Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week subject to:
  - Role eligibility (confirmed by HR)
  - Manager approval
  - Minimum 6 months service
  - Completion of the Remote Work Agreement form

3.3 Attendance
Employees must notify their manager before 09:00 on any day of absence.
Repeated unexplained absences may result in disciplinary action.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. RESIGNATION & SEPARATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Resignation Procedure
Employees wishing to resign must:
  1. Submit written notice to their line manager and HR via email.
  2. Provide the notice period as per their employment contract.

Notice Periods:
  - 0–1 year of service:   2 weeks notice
  - 1–3 years of service:  4 weeks notice
  - 3–5 years of service:  6 weeks notice
  - 5+ years of service:   8 weeks notice

4.2 Exit Interview
All departing employees are invited to attend an exit interview
with HR within 5 business days of resignation submission.

4.3 Final Pay & Benefits
Final salary (including accrued but untaken holiday) is paid on the
last standard payroll date after the employee's final working day.

4.4 Return of Company Property
Employees must return all company property (laptop, access cards, etc.)
on or before their last working day. Failure to do so may result in
deductions from final pay.

5. CODE OF CONDUCT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 Professional Behaviour
All employees are expected to behave professionally, honestly, and
with respect toward colleagues, clients, and partners.

5.2 Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to
their manager and the Ethics hotline within 5 business days.

5.3 Confidentiality
Employees must not share company confidential information
externally without written authorisation from their VP or above.

5.4 Social Media
Employees may not post content that could damage ACME's reputation
or disclose proprietary information. Personal opinions must be
clearly marked as not representing ACME.

6. DISCIPLINARY POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Disciplinary action follows a progressive structure:
  Step 1: Verbal Warning (documented in HR system)
  Step 2: Written Warning
  Step 3: Final Written Warning
  Step 4: Termination

Gross misconduct (fraud, harassment, data theft) may result in
immediate termination without prior warnings.

7. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Employees with grievances should:
  1. Discuss informally with their line manager.
  2. If unresolved, submit a formal grievance form to HR.
  3. HR will convene a hearing within 10 business days.
  4. Employees may be accompanied by a colleague or union rep.
  5. Decision communicated in writing within 5 business days.

8. BENEFITS SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Health Insurance: ACME covers 90% of employee premiums (70% for dependants).
Dental & Vision: Included in the standard plan.
Annual Leave: 25 days per year (increasing by 1 day per year up to 30 days).
Sick Leave: 15 days per year (non-cumulative).
Pension/401k: ACME matches contributions up to 5% of base salary.
Life Insurance: 3x annual salary.
Learning Budget: $2,000 per year per employee for approved training.
Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 24/7 counselling and support.
