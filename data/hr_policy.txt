1. LEAVE POLICIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 Annual Leave (Vacation)
Full-time employees receive:
  - Years 1–3:   25 days per year
  - Years 3–5:   27 days per year
  - Years 5+:    30 days per year

Leave must be requested via the HR portal at least 5 business days
in advance (2 weeks for leave exceeding 10 days).
Unused leave may be carried over up to 10 days to the next calendar year.
All unused leave above 10 days is forfeited on December 31.

1.2 Sick Leave
  - 15 days per year (non-cumulative, does not carry over)
  - Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
  - Sick leave during approved annual leave does not count as sick leave

1.3 Parental Leave
  - Primary Carer (birth/adoption): 26 weeks fully paid, then 13 weeks 60% pay
  - Secondary Carer:                4 weeks fully paid
  - Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
  - Requires 4 weeks advance notice (or as soon as practicable)

1.4 Bereavement Leave
  - Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days paid
  - Extended family (grandparent, in-law):             3 days paid
  - Close friend/colleague (at manager discretion):    1 day

1.5 Compassionate Leave
Granted case-by-case basis. Apply via HR portal with manager approval.

1.6 Public Holidays
ACME observes all federal/national public holidays in the employee's
country of employment. A full list is published on the intranet.
Employees required to work on a public holiday receive 1.5x pay
or an alternative day off (employee's choice).

1.7 Unpaid Leave
Available after 12 months of employment. Maximum 3 months.
Requires VP approval. Benefits suspended during unpaid leave.

2. PURCHASE ORDER & PROCUREMENT POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 Approval Thresholds (Single Purchase)
  - Up to $500:         Line Manager approval
  - $501 – $5,000:      Line Manager + Finance BP approval
  - $5,001 – $25,000:   Director approval
  - $25,001 – $100,000: VP approval
  - Over $100,000:      CFO + CEO approval

2.2 Process
  1. Raise a Purchase Request in the Procurement system.
  2. Obtain approvals as per thresholds above.
  3. Finance issues the official Purchase Order.
  4. Supplier confirms PO; delivery tracked in system.
  5. Goods received note (GRN) triggers payment.

2.3 Preferred Suppliers
Always use pre-approved suppliers where available.
New supplier onboarding requires Finance BP approval and
a Supplier Due Diligence form (minimum 10 business days).

3. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Performance Review Cycle
  - Quarterly Check-ins:  Manager and employee (informal)
  - Mid-Year Review:      June–July (formal, documented)
  - Annual Review:        December (formal, links to compensation)

3.2 Performance Ratings
  1 — Exceptional:    Top 10% performers; accelerated progression
  2 — Exceeds:        Top 20%; eligible for merit increase > 5%
  3 — Meets:          On-track employees; standard merit increase
  4 — Below:          Requires Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
  5 — Unsatisfactory: At risk of termination; immediate PIP

3.3 Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
A PIP is a 60-day structured plan with clear, measurable goals.
If goals are not met by day 60, employment may be terminated.

4. COMPENSATION & PAYROLL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Pay Frequency
  - US employees:        Bi-weekly (every other Friday)
  - International:       Monthly (last business day of month)

4.2 Merit Increases
Effective April 1 each year, subject to budget approval:
  - Rating 1 (Exceptional):   8–12% increase
  - Rating 2 (Exceeds):       5–8% increase
  - Rating 3 (Meets):         2–4% increase
  - Rating 4/5:               No increase; PIP active

4.3 Bonus Structure
Annual bonus paid in March, based on:
  - Company performance (40%)
  - Department performance (30%)
  - Individual performance (30%)
Target bonus ranges from 5% (IC) to 20% (Director+) of base salary.

4.4 Pay Transparency
ACME publishes salary bands for all roles on the internal intranet.
Employees may discuss compensation openly without fear of retaliation.

5. LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 Annual Learning Budget
Each employee receives $2,000 per calendar year for:
  - External courses and certifications
  - Conference attendance
  - Books and learning materials
  - Online learning platforms (Coursera, Udemy, etc.)

5.2 Internal Training
ACME Academy offers 200+ online courses on business, technical, and
leadership topics. All courses are free and self-paced.

5.3 Tuition Reimbursement
Employees pursuing a work-related degree may apply for up to
$5,000/year reimbursement (A/B grade required; 2-year service bond).
