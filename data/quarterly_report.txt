EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ACME Corporation achieved record quarterly revenue in Q2 2025, driven by
strong enterprise AI product sales and expansion in the APAC region.
Total revenue reached $487.2M, representing 18.4% year-over-year growth
compared to Q2 2024 ($411.5M). Operating margin improved to 24.3% (Q2 2024: 21.8%).

Key headlines:
  ✅ Revenue: $487.2M (+18.4% YoY)
  ✅ Operating Income: $118.4M (+25.1% YoY)
  ✅ EBITDA: $141.6M (29.1% margin)
  ✅ Net Income: $89.3M (+22.7% YoY)
  ✅ EPS: $1.84 (vs. $1.50 in Q2 2024)
  ⚠️  Cash Position: $342.1M (down 8.2% from Q1 due to acquisitions)
  ✅ Headcount: 28,412 (net adds: +847 in Q2)
