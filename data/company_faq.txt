Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to password.acmecorp.internal and follow the self-service reset.
   If you are locked out, call IT Helpdesk on Ext. 4001 (24/7).
   Passwords must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase,
   number, and symbol. Passwords expire every 90 days.

Q: How do I connect to the VPN?
A: Download GlobalProtect VPN from the IT portal. Use your ACME domain
   credentials. MFA is required. Contact IT if you have connection issues.

Q: My laptop is not working. What do I do?
A: Raise a ticket at helpdesk.acmecorp.internal or call Ext. 4001.
   For hardware failures, a loaner laptop can be arranged within 4 hours.

Q: Can I use personal devices for work?
A: Yes, through the BYOD programme. Enroll your device at
   mdm.acmecorp.internal. The ACME MDM profile will be installed.
   Personal data and work data are kept separate.

Q: How do I access the company intranet?
A: Visit intranet.acmecorp.internal from any browser while on VPN.

SECTION B: HR & PAYROLL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: When is payday?
A: US employees are paid bi-weekly on Fridays. International employees
   are paid on the last business day of each month.

Q: How do I view my payslip?
A: Log in to Workday (workday.acmecorp.internal) → Payroll → Payslips.
   Payslips are available 2 business days before payday.

Q: How do I request annual leave?
A: Submit leave requests via Workday → Time Off → Request.
   Allow 5 business days for manager approval.
   For leave longer than 10 days, allow 2 weeks notice.

Q: Who is my HR Business Partner?
A: Your HRBP is listed in Workday under Team → HR Contact.
   You can also email hr@acmecorp.internal for general queries.

Q: How do I update my personal details?
A: Log in to Workday and update under Personal → Personal Information.
   For legal name changes, you must submit supporting documentation to HR.

Q: What is the EAP (Employee Assistance Programme)?
A: The EAP provides free, confidential counselling and support services.
   Available 24/7 at eap.acmecorp.internal or call 1-800-ACME-HELP.
   Services include mental health, financial advice, and legal guidance.

Q: How do I submit an expense report?
A: Submit via Concur (concur.acmecorp.internal). Attach all receipts.
   Reports must be submitted within 30 days of travel.
   For help, contact travel@acmecorp.internal.

Q: What is the purchase order process?
A: Raise a PR in the Procurement system. Your manager approves.
   Finance reviews and issues a PO. Refer to HR Policy Manual
   Section 2 for approval thresholds.

Q: How do I get a corporate credit card?
A: Apply through Finance BP if you travel more than 3 times per year.
   Contact finance-na@acmecorp.internal for the application form.

Q: When are invoices paid?
A: Standard payment terms are Net 30. Expedited payment (Net 7)
   is available with Finance Director approval.

Q: How do I book a meeting room?
A: Use the Room Booking system in Outlook Calendar or
   at rooms.acmecorp.internal. Rooms auto-release if not checked in
   within 15 minutes of the booking start time.

Q: How do I get an office access card?
A: New employees receive access cards from Facilities on Day 1.
   If you lose your card, report immediately to security@acmecorp.internal
   and Facilities (Ext. 4020). Replacement takes 1 business day.

Q: Where can I park?
A: Parking permits are issued on a first-come basis. Apply via
   facilities.acmecorp.internal. Monthly cost is subsidised at $75/month
   (pre-tax deducted from payroll).

Q: Is there a cafeteria?
A: Yes, cafeteria is open 07:30–15:00 Monday to Friday.
   Subsidised meals are available. Dietary options labelled clearly.

Q: How do I access my learning budget?
A: Submit a Learning Request in Workday → Learning → Development.
   Obtain line manager approval. Finance BP approves claims over $500.
   Receipts required for reimbursement.

Q: What certifications does ACME sponsor?
A: ACME sponsors AWS, Azure, GCP, PMP, CISSP, and AI/ML certifications.
   Full list at learning.acmecorp.internal. First attempt fully paid;
   retakes require manager approval.

Q: Can I attend external conferences?
A: Yes, using your $2,000 learning budget. Registration + travel expenses
   must be pre-approved via the Travel Portal.

Q: How do I report a security incident?
A: Immediately email security@acmecorp.internal or call Ext. 4030.
   For data breaches, contact the CISO directly at ciso@acmecorp.internal.
   Do not delay — regulatory notification deadlines apply.

Q: What should I do in an emergency at the office?
A: Follow the Emergency Evacuation Plan posted near all exits.
   Fire assembly point is the car park on Level B1.
   First aid kits are located at every reception desk.

Q: How do I report unethical behaviour?
A: Use the Ethics Hotline (anonymous): 1-800-ACME-ETH or
   ethics@acmecorp.internal. All reports are investigated confidentially.
