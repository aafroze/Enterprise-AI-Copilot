ACME CORPORATION — TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective: March 1, 2025
Finance & Operations | Confidential

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. PURPOSE & SCOPE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

This policy governs all business travel and expense reimbursement for
ACME Corporation employees globally. It applies to all full-time,
part-time, and contract employees travelling on company business.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. TRAVEL APPROVAL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 All business travel must be approved BEFORE booking:
  - Domestic travel (< $500):     Line Manager approval
  - Domestic travel ($500–$2000): Line Manager + Finance BP approval
  - International travel:         VP-level approval required
  - Executive travel (C-Suite):   CFO approval required

2.2 Travel requests must be submitted via the Travel Portal at least:
  - Domestic: 5 business days in advance
  - International: 15 business days in advance

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. FLIGHTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Class of Travel
  - Flights under 4 hours:    Economy class only
  - Flights 4–8 hours:        Premium Economy (VP+ may use Business)
  - Flights over 8 hours:     Business class (Economy for IC employees)
  - First class:              Never reimbursed, regardless of role

3.2 Booking
All flights must be booked through the approved corporate travel
portal (Concur) or via the travel helpdesk. Personal bookings
require pre-approval and are reimbursed at economy equivalent rate.

3.3 Frequent Flyer Miles
Employees may retain personal frequent flyer miles earned from
business travel, provided the flights are booked at standard rates.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. ACCOMMODATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Hotel Limits (per night, room rate only)
  - Tier 1 Cities (NYC, London, SF, Tokyo, Singapore): $250/night
  - Tier 2 Cities (Major metros):                      $180/night
  - Tier 3 Cities (Secondary):                         $120/night

4.2 Extended Stays
For assignments exceeding 14 nights, employees should use a
company-negotiated serviced apartment. Contact Facilities.

4.3 Airbnb/Private Rentals
Permitted up to the equivalent hotel rate limit for that city tier.
Receipts required. Personal safety is the employee's responsibility.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. MEALS & DAILY ALLOWANCES (PER DIEM)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5.1 Per Diem Rates
  - Domestic (USA):         $75/day
  - International — EMEA:  $90/day
  - International — APAC:  $85/day
  - International — LATAM: $70/day

5.2 Meals with Clients
Business meals with clients or external partners may be claimed
above per diem limits, subject to:
  - Pre-approval from line manager
  - Receipt + list of attendees + business purpose required
  - Maximum $150 per person for client meals
  - Alcohol: Maximum $30 per person, must be shown separately

5.3 Alcohol Policy
Alcohol may only be claimed at client entertainment events
(not internal meals) and within the $30/person limit.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. GROUND TRANSPORT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6.1 Airport Transfers
  - Company car/driver: Approved for VP and above
  - Taxi/Uber/Lyft: Reimbursed at actual cost with receipt
  - Public transport: Preferred where practical; fully reimbursed

6.2 Rental Cars
  - Permitted when public transport is impractical
  - Standard category only (no SUV unless 4+ passengers)
  - Always purchase CDW/LDW insurance through the portal

6.3 Personal Vehicle
  - Reimbursed at IRS standard mileage rate ($0.67/mile for 2025)
  - Submit mileage log with start/end odometer readings

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
7. EXPENSE SUBMISSION & REIMBURSEMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7.1 Submission Deadline
All expense reports must be submitted within 30 calendar days
of travel completion. Late submissions may be declined.

7.2 Required Documentation
  - Original receipts for all expenses over $25
  - Business purpose stated clearly on each line item
  - Manager approval signature or digital approval in Concur

7.3 Reimbursement Timeline
  - Standard processing: 10 business days
  - Expedited (with Finance BP approval): 3 business days

7.4 Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following are NEVER reimbursed:
  - Personal entertainment (cinema, sports events, spa)
  - Traffic/parking fines and tolls (unless on approved route)
  - Airline seat upgrades not pre-approved
  - Minibar charges
  - Personal phone calls
  - Pet care
  - Lost or stolen items
  - Credit card fees or interest

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
8. CORPORATE CREDIT CARD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Corporate cards are issued to employees who travel more than
3 times per year. Cardholders must:
  - Submit expense reports by the 5th of each month
  - Never use the card for personal expenses
  - Report a lost card within 4 hours

9. POLICY VIOLATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Repeated policy violations result in suspension of travel privileges.
